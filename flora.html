Flora & Fauna

Learn about the rich biodiversity of Panna Tiger Reserve

flora

The reserve has tropical dry deciduous forests dominated by teak, bamboo.

  • Money Plant(Epipremnum aurrum)
  • Water hyacinth
  • Bamboo
  • Neem Tree(Azadirachta indica)
  • Hibiscus
  • Rose
  • Tulsi
  • Aloevera

Fauna
